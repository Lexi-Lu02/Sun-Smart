💡 Myth Busted

"Cloudy days are safe."

UV rays penetrate up to 80% through clouds — you can still get severely burned even when it feels cool or overcast.

💡 Myth Busted

"I never burn, so I don't need SPF."

Tanning IS sun damage. DNA damage begins well before redness appears — no visible burn doesn't mean no harm.

💡 Myth Busted

"A tan makes you look healthy."

A tan is your skin's stress response to UV damage — it's a sign of injury, not health. Premature ageing begins immediately.
